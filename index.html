Fear Of Sharp Things

In Viet Tri, Jul 13, 2020

Aichmophobia is a phobia of sharp, pointed objects. Those affected by aichmophobia will feel anxious, worried, and fearful around any object that is sharp and could cause harm. This could include pencils, pens, needles, pins, scissors, and other common household items. People with trypanophobia solely fear needles and medical procedures involving needles. People with belonephobia fear pins and needles specifically, while people with aichmophobia fear many types of sharp, pointed objects.
