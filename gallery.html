OUR WORK

Hello, BUMBLEBEE's gallery is here!

I, Luke, Liliah and Luna have met and worked together since 2017. We want to tell stories through photos, not only words, images also have tremendous power. “A picture is worth a thousand words.” That is the motto of my team. It is our objective to produce direct, truthful and bold images that tell the stories for those who have no voice.

Her name is Anna

Anna was the name of my first camera, it was a gift that my parents gave me on my 15th birthday.

I currently no longer use Anna, exactly as I do not use Anna regularly for work. I think she's an old age, and I'm pretty clumsy, I'm afraid of dropping her, and I think she should get some rest. I remember the days of spring, summer, autumn and winter since I was 15, I have always worn Anna on my neck and the two of us went to many places together. My first partner on the path of photography, Anna, she's a special friend.
