About Us

We tell stories through photos, no words, just the photographs.


Me

Shmily Blossom


I pretty much never pick up my camera out of a sense of duty. I pick it up because I want to document something or capture something. I take pictures for... the sheer joy of it. Photography is a creative outlet for me. As always, lalala happy shooting! I take photos to freeze a beautiful moment and capture it forever. I see beauty in many people who don't see it in themselves and my goal as a photographer is to show them their own beauty. I also find photography to be an art form and think of it as just as relaxing as sitting down with my sketch pad. There are many terrible things in the world and I believe photography is an art that can help remind people of all the wonder in the world.
